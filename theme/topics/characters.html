Master Chief (John)

Master Chief is the main protagonist in the Halo series. Being a spartan he has saved countless lives and is considered one of the best soldier in the UNSC (United Nations Space Command). Being a part of the war for most of his life, he is very seasonsed and is often cold to others. Although he is cold he does care for others safety more than anything. Along with this attitude he is typically very quiet and likes to work alone. He can be described as a lone wolf, almost seeing others as potentially in the way.

The Arbiter

The partner(former) of Master Chief. This alien believes what the Covenant are doing is wrong. His eyes have been opened and he has started a resistance in stopping them. His small resistance of the used to be Covenant have teamed up with the UNSC in order to stop the main teorrist force. Later in the series he breaks away from the UNSC in order to persue his own goals as they don't completetly align with the UNSC. While the UNSC go off to scatter and finish off the Covenant, the Arbiter and his small band go back to their home planet to take it back.

Cortana (AI)

Master Chief's Artifical Intelligence. This AI was given to Master Chief when he first started his program as a spartan. This AI is sentient and is essentially another human but as a robot. She is placed in a small chip which Master Chief puts in his armor. Cortana is able to talk with Chief through his helmet and make calculations like any normal robot. Their relationship with each other has strengthened to the point of become very close friends.
